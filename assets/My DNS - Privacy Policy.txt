What data we collect
My DNS collects only the minimum data necessary to operate its features.

Location (Wi-Fi network name only)
When you grant location permission, My DNS reads your Wi-Fi network name (SSID) to support the Exclusive Wi-Fi feature. No GPS coordinates or any other location data are collected or transmitted. This data never leaves your device.

Speed test data
Speed tests are powered by the open-source LibreSpeed engine. When a test runs, the selected test server may record:
• Test results (download speed, upload speed, ping, jitter)
• ISP information and approximate location inferred from your IP address
• User-agent string and app locale
• A test log containing no personal information
IP addresses are not stored by test servers. My DNS does not generate or store any test identifiers.

How we use the data
Data stored on your device (Wi-Fi network names, DNS preferences) is used exclusively to power app features and never leaves your device. Speed test results transmitted to test servers are governed by the policies of those servers, over which My DNS has no control.

Third-party services
Speed tests connect to independent third-party servers. My DNS is not responsible for the data practices of those servers.

Push notifications
My DNS may send push notifications to improve your experience. You can disable them at any time from the app settings or your device settings.

Changes to this policy
The Owner reserves the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We recommend checking this page periodically.
